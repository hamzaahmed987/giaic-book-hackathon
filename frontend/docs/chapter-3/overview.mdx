# Chapter 3: Prompt Engineering

<ChapterActions chapterId="chapter-3" />

## Learning Objectives

:::info Objectives
1. **Master** prompt design principles and patterns
2. **Apply** techniques like few-shot learning and chain-of-thought
3. **Optimize** prompts for specific tasks and outcomes
4. **Debug** and iterate on underperforming prompts
:::

## Why This Chapter Matters

The quality of your prompts directly determines the quality of AI outputs. Prompt engineering is the skill that separates effective AI users from frustrated ones.
